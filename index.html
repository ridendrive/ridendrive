About Us

At Ride N Drive Transportation, we are a reliable non-emergency medical transportation service (NEMT). Our team is made up of drivers with the experience, knowledge, and compassion to ensure a pleasant experience.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, safe, and compassionate transportation to seniors and individuals of all needs.

Our Core Values

  • Safety
    Passenger well-being always comes first.
  • Compassion
    We acknowledge the specific needs and aspirations of each individual we serve, and our team is devoted to providing compassionate care that caters to both physical and emotional needs on and off road.
  • Reliability
    On-time service you can trust.
  • Professionalism
    Trained, courteous staff.
  • Accessibility
    Vehicles equipped for all needs.
